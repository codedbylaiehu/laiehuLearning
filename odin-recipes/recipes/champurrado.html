Champuladu

My favorite noodle dish!

Prep Time: 5mins Cook Time: 45mins.

It’s a porridge of sorts, only made with rice, evaporated milk and CHOCOLATE! I think that’s why I love it so much — who doesn’t love chocolate? Author: Annie @ Annie's Chamorro Kitchen.

Ingredients

    1 cup uncooked rice, or 2 cups for a thicker “porridge” 4 cups water
  • 1 can evaporated milk
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup sugar

Instructions

  1. Wash the rice and place in a medium sized pot. Add water and cook over medium high heat. Keep the pot lit on until the rice begins to boil.
  2. Once the rice comes to a boil, reduce the heat to mediumlow; add cocoa powder, sugar, and milk.
  3. Whisk to combine all the ingredients. Continue to cook at a low simmer over medium-low heat for approximately 30 more minutes. The mixture should thicken considerably during this time. If it’s too thick for your liking, add more milk then adjust the sugar to taste. Serve warm and enjoy!
